my virtualAdventure

This exciting Product will provide you the most immersible adventurous virtual trip that anybody could have to any part of the World. Get ready for the next best thing ever happened in the field of virtual Adventure Tourism

The Product offering is aimed at tapping into the vast emerging market of adventure tourism. It will involve live streaming of video images, delivered to Consumers sensory system. For maximum impact a specific "Head Mounted Display"(HMD) device (e.g., Sony's HMD devices) will be designed, which will deliver near live audio visual effect to an individual's Sensory system.

The live streaming will be done thru Internet connected Cameras (e.g., Google's 360 degrees Trakker Camera) being carried by specialist Guides familiar with specific terrains in remote exotic locations around the World. The content delivery could be both live and pre-recorded based on two different pricing tiers. The Streaming of the content will be done over the existing high-speed broadband network of a country. The marketing of the product, will initially follow the 'On Demand' model of screening of movies as currently done by cable TV networks.

There will be four distinct components to the whole venture:

  • The Company will be responsible for creation of near real-time content generation from Live Adventure trips to different parts of the World.
  • The company will either 'white label' an existing third party product or negotiate with a third party of repute to manufacture the 'Trakker Cameras' and the HMD devices.
  • Content Distribution will happen over existing commercial cable network.
  • An autonomous R&D facility will be established in co-operation with specific University and Research institutes to investigate into 'Human Visualization' and other Sensory functions. Continuous innovation thru R&D effort would be the main 'mantra' of the Company.
